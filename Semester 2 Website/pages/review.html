Mary - Dublin (June, 2015)

I was very impressed with Lakeside House and the town of Killarney, in which it is located. Gerry and Siobhan were extremely personable hosts and made me feel quite at home. The bed was quite comfortable and the bathroom both immaculate and modern. The central kitchen and living room area in which guests can all converge was warm and hospitable. Gerry gave me some excellent recommendations to grab a pint of Guinness and for a terrific local meal. He also provided helpful tips of sights to see. The room was fairly priced and I would highly recommend Lakeside House to anyone looking to stay in Killarney. Some friends had suggested staying in scenic Killarney over edgy Kerry and I am glad that they provided this suggestion.
